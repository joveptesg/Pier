What is this?

Federation works in two directions. This tab is about the incoming direction — giving another pier-core the right to control this one.

  • Outgoing (controlling other servers from here) is managed on the Servers page via the "Peer core" kind.
  • Incoming is here: you issue a token and hand it to the operator of another pier-core. They paste it into their own "Add Server → Peer core" form, and their panel gains admin-equivalent access to yours over REST.

Tokens grant full admin rights — revoke any time; this core keeps working autonomously either way.

Protocol: requests authenticated with the X-Pier-Peer-Token header match a row in peer_grants and are treated as an admin session for that call.
