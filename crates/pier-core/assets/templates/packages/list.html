Configure your client

In your project's .npmrc:

registry=https://YOUR-PIER-HOST/registry/npm/
//YOUR-PIER-HOST/registry/npm/:_authToken=pier_npm_xxx
always-auth=true

always-auth=true is required for yarn 1.x (it skips the Bearer token on GET otherwise). npm, pnpm, and bun ignore it harmlessly.

Mirror public packages from an upstream registry.

When enabled, a request for a package not yet in this registry transparently fetches the packument from the upstream URL (defaults to registry.npmjs.org), caches it locally with is_proxy = 1, and lazily downloads tarballs on first access. Private publishes are unaffected — they always win over a proxy entry.

Pick an S3-compatible storage to mirror published tarballs to. Tarballs are still served from the local FS at low latency — S3 is the durability backstop and lets you re-hydrate after a VPS reinstall. Leave unset to disable mirroring.
