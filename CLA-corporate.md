# Corporate Contributor License Agreement

This Corporate Contributor License Agreement ("Agreement") documents the rights
granted by a contributing organization ("Corporation") to the maintainers of
Pier. It exists for the common case where contributions are made by employees
whose work — and therefore copyright — belongs to their employer (for example,
"work made for hire"). In that situation an individual cannot grant the rights in
the [Individual CLA](CLA.md), because the individual does not own the copyright;
the Corporation that owns it must grant them.

> **Contributing your own work as an individual?** Sign the
> [Individual CLA](CLA.md) instead.

By signing this Agreement — and by its authorized employees submitting
contributions — the Corporation agrees to the following terms for its present and
future contributions to Pier.

### 1. Definitions

- **"Corporation"** means the entity that owns the copyright in the Contributions
  and that is entering into this Agreement.
- **"You" / "Your"** means the Corporation.
- **"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship, including
  modifications or additions to existing work, submitted to the Project by the
  Corporation or by an employee or agent authorized by the Corporation (see
  Schedule A).
- **"Project"** means the Pier software and all associated repositories.

### 2. Grant of Copyright License

The Corporation hereby grants to the project maintainers a perpetual, worldwide,
non-exclusive, no-charge, royalty-free, irrevocable copyright license to
reproduce, prepare derivative works of, publicly display, publicly perform,
sublicense, and distribute its Contributions and such derivative works.

### 3. Grant of Patent License

The Corporation hereby grants to the project maintainers a perpetual, worldwide,
non-exclusive, no-charge, royalty-free, irrevocable patent license to make, have
made, use, offer to sell, sell, import, and otherwise transfer the Project, where
such license applies only to those patent claims licensable by the Corporation
that are necessarily infringed by its Contribution(s) alone or by combination of
its Contribution(s) with the Project.

### 4. Representations

The Corporation represents that:

- It is legally entitled to grant the above licenses.
- Each Contribution is either an original creation of the Corporation or its
  authorized employees, or the Corporation otherwise has the right to submit it
  under these terms.
- The person signing this Agreement is authorized to bind the Corporation.
- It will notify the project maintainers if any listed employee should no longer
  be authorized, or if any representation becomes inaccurate.

### 5. Dual Licensing

The Corporation understands and agrees that the project maintainers may offer the
Project under additional license terms (dual licensing), including commercial
licenses, and that its Contributions may be included in such offerings.

### 6. No Obligation

The Corporation understands that the decision to include any Contribution in the
Project is entirely at the discretion of the project maintainers.
